What We Do?

Our services are specifically tailored to meet your accounting and taxation requirements.

  • GST compliance

    We provide comprehensive GST services, including registration and monthly/quarterly filing such as GSTR3B, GSTR1, GSTR-4, and GSTR-9.

  • Balance Sheet

    Our expertise extends to preparing Balance Sheets for various entity types, encompassing Proprietorship firms, Partnership firms, LLPs, and Private Limited companies.

  • EPF and ESIC Filing

    We specialize in the filing of Provident Fund (PF) and Employee's State Insurance Corporation (ESIC).

  • Accounting

    We manage daily accounting tasks: trial balance, invoicing, vouchers, payable/receivable, bill verification, and billing processes.

  • Income Tax Return (ITR) Filing

    Our specialization lies in filing Income Tax Returns (ITRs) for businesses and individuals with both salary and business income.

  • MSME/Udyam Registration

    We specialize in facilitating the registration process for MSME (Micro, Small, Medium Enterprises) and updating existing MSME profiles.

  • TDS

    We specialize in TAN registration, TDS return filing, and calculation services.

  • Trademark

    We specialize in trademark registration and provide our clients with a hassle-free trademarking experience.
